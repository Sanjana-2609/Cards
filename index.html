Lion

The Lion is large cat native to Africa and central Iran. It is the Fastestland animal, capable of running at 80 to 128 km/h (50 to 80 mph).

Mandrill

The Mandrill is a primate of the Old World monkey family. It is one of two species assigned to the genus Mandrillus, along with the drill.

White Tiger

The White Tiger (also known as the White Bengal Tiger) is a subspecies of Tiger, found throughout the indian subcontinent
